        Why Clickshame?

        You know that sting of annoyance and anger you get when you accidentally click on clickbait? Clickshame was born deep within a sloppy cesspool of those feelings.

        Nobody likes clickbait. It is a waste of time, it makes you feel tricked and deceived. At its worst it can spread lies and false outrage across the internet and even ruin lives. We created clickshame.com to continue the discussion about clickbait on the internet. This isn't the first time society has had a problem with yellow journalism. Clickshame is not meant to be the whole solution, but we hope that it will be a catalyst. A catalyst for innovation in the online media industry.

        Our hope is that new business models can be discovered and implemented that reward quality content over deceptive and cheap tricks for clicks.

        You can help by participating and sharing. The more people who regularly use Clickshame, the more powerful it will be as a force for change.
